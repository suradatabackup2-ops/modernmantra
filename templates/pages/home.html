Who We Are

Slow Travel.
Real Stories.
Deep Connections.

ModernMantra began as a small farmstay tucked in the hills above Manali — a place where strangers arrived and left as friends. Today, we curate slow, meaningful journeys across India and beyond with small groups, offbeat places, and experiences that bring people closer to nature and to each other.

From snowy Himalayan passes to sun-soaked beaches, from ancient monasteries to vibrant city streets — we design every trip to be lived, not just toured.
